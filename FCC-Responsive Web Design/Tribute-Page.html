Arsene who?

Arsene Wenger is a french born association football manager and player. He managed Arsenal football club from 1996 to 2018 and their longest serving manager and the most successful one. He also managed A.S.Monaco, a french first division team and J.league team Naogoya Grampus Eight in his long prestigious career. Arsene led the Arsenal football club to league double in his first complete season a feat he soon achieved in 2001-02 season. His most important accomplishment in his career as Arsenal football manager was to win the league without losing a single match in the season of 2003-04, a feat last accomplished by Preston North End, 115 years previously. The team were promptly named as invincibles and awarded a golden replica of the league trophy.

Wenger's teams through ages are known for their free flowing attacking football. He is a football visionary and played a key role in shaping the modern Arsenal football history. He oversaw them transition from their old Highbury stadium which constrained the club financially but kept the club competitive though was unable to win the league after the move.
